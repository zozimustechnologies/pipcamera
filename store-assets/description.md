# PiP Camera — Your Rear-View Mirror for Work

## Never get surprised by your manager again. 😎

You know the feeling. You're deep in the zone — headphones on, crushing it — and then *tap tap* on the shoulder. Your soul leaves your body. Your manager is standing right behind you. How long have they been there? Nobody knows.

**PiP Camera fixes that.** It's a tiny, always-on-top camera feed that floats in the corner of your screen like a rear-view mirror for your desk. You see everything behind you. No more jump scares. No more panic-closing tabs. Just calm, confident awareness.

---

## How It Works

PiP Camera uses your webcam and the browser's built-in **Picture-in-Picture** mode to float a live video feed on top of everything. It stays visible while you code, write, browse, present, or pretend to look busy — no extra apps, no extra windows.

---

## Why You'll Love It

- 🚀 **One-Click Launch** — Click the icon, camera starts. That's it. No setup, no config.
- 📌 **Always on Top** — The floating PiP window stays visible across all tabs and windows. You'll never miss someone sneaking up.
- 🔒 **100% Private** — Nothing leaves your device. No cloud, no servers, no tracking, no analytics. Your feed lives and dies in the browser.
- 🪶 **Featherweight** — Barely uses any CPU or memory. Zero background processes when the camera is off.
- 🎨 **Clean UI** — A calm, earthy blue interface that looks good and stays out of your way.
- 🛑 **Full Control** — Start, stop, enter PiP, or exit PiP with big, obvious buttons. No confusion.

---

## How to Use

1. **Pin PiP Camera** to your Edge toolbar.
2. **Click the icon** — camera page opens and your webcam starts automatically.
3. **Hit "Enter PiP"** — a floating window pops out in the corner.
4. **Drag it anywhere** — resize it, tuck it in a corner, put it wherever feels right.
5. Done for the day? **"Stop Camera"** and it's gone. No lingering processes.
