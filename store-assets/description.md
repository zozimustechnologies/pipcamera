# PiP Camera — Your Rear-View Camera for the Workspace

## Stay Aware. Stay Focused.

Ever been startled by someone walking up behind you while you're deep in work? **PiP Camera** puts a small, always-on-top camera feed in the corner of your screen — like a rear-view mirror for your desk. One click and you'll never be caught off guard again.

---

## What It Does

PiP Camera uses your device's webcam and the browser's built-in **Picture-in-Picture** API to float a live camera feed on top of every window. It stays visible while you type, code, browse, or present — no extra apps, no extra windows.

---

## Key Features

- **One-Click Launch** — Click the toolbar icon, and your camera starts automatically.
- **Picture-in-Picture Mode** — A floating, resizable, always-on-top video feed that stays visible across all tabs and windows.
- **Start / Stop Anytime** — Full control to start, stop, enter, or exit PiP with clearly labeled buttons.
- **Privacy First** — No data leaves your device. No cloud. No servers. No tracking. Your camera feed lives entirely in the browser.
- **Lightweight** — Minimal CPU and memory usage. No background processes when the camera is off.
- **Clean, Modern UI** — A calm, earthy blue interface that doesn't distract from your work.

---

## How to Use

1. **Pin the extension** to your Edge toolbar for quick access.
2. **Click the PiP Camera icon** — the camera page opens and your webcam starts automatically.
3. **Click "Enter PiP"** — your camera feed pops out into a small floating window.
4. **Move and resize** the PiP window anywhere on your screen.
5. **Click "Exit PiP"** to return to the full view, or **"Stop Camera"** to turn it off completely.
