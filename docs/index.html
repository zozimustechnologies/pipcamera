How It Works

Up and running in seconds.

1

Pin the Extension

Pin PiP Camera to your Edge toolbar for quick access.

2

Click the Icon

The camera page opens and your webcam starts automatically.

3

Enter PiP

A floating window pops out. Drag it anywhere, resize it, tuck it in a corner.

4

Stay Aware

Work normally with your rear-view mirror keeping you informed.
